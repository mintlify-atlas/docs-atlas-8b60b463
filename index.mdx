Whether you're exploring a local SQLite file or querying a production PostgreSQL cluster, Beekeeper gives you a clean, modern interface without sacrificing power.

## Quick start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Beekeeper Studio">
    Visit [beekeeperstudio.io](https://beekeeperstudio.io/get-community) and download the installer for your operating system — macOS, Windows, or Linux.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the application">
    Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions. See [installation guides](/installation/index) for platform-specific details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a connection">
    Click **New Connection**, select your database type, enter your credentials, and click **Connect**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start exploring">
    Browse tables in the sidebar, open the SQL editor, and start querying your data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Beekeeper Studio is free to download with no sign-up or credit card required. Premium features are available with a paid subscription. [Compare editions](/support/editions) to learn more.
</Note>
