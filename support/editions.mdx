Beekeeper Studio is a single download with in-app upgrades for premium features — you never need to reinstall the app to unlock the paid edition. The Community edition is completely free, open source under the GPLv3 license, and requires no sign-up or credit card. The Ultimate edition adds advanced features and database support through a commercial subscription.

    [Download Community](https://github.com/beekeeper-studio/beekeeper-studio/releases/latest)

## Upgrading from Community to Ultimate

You do not need to download a new app to upgrade. Simply purchase a license key and enter it into the app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Purchase a license">
    Visit the [pricing page](https://beekeeperstudio.io/pricing) and choose a plan that fits your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your license key">
    After your purchase completes, you'll receive a license name and license key by email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the key in the app">
    Open Beekeeper Studio, click **Help → Manage License Keys**, and enter your license key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using Ultimate features">
    Premium features and additional databases unlock immediately — no restart required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Note>

## Open source commitment

A significant portion of Beekeeper Studio's source code is [available on GitHub](https://github.com/beekeeper-studio/beekeeper-studio) under the GPLv3 license. The Community edition is made up entirely of open source code. All purchases of the Ultimate edition directly support continued investment in the open source Community edition.
