---
title: "Purchasing a Beekeeper Studio License and Subscription"
description: "Learn how to buy a Beekeeper Studio license, activate it in the app, manage your subscription, and understand lifetime access and renewal discounts."
---

Beekeeper Studio licenses are sold as subscriptions — monthly or annual — from the [pricing page](https://beekeeperstudio.io/pricing). Licenses are issued to **people**, not machines, so purchase one license per individual user. Subscriptions include perpetual lifetime access to any version released during your paid period, and you get a 20% renewal discount once you've been subscribed for 12 months.

## How to purchase

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the pricing page">
    Visit [beekeeperstudio.io/pricing](https://beekeeperstudio.io/pricing) and compare available plans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan and billing cycle">
    Select monthly or annual billing. Annual subscribers receive lifetime access immediately. Monthly subscribers receive lifetime access after completing 12 consecutive months.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    Enter your payment details. Beekeeper Studio uses Stripe for secure payment processing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your license key">
    After your purchase completes, you'll receive a license name and license key by email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate in the app">
    Open Beekeeper Studio, click **Help → Manage License Keys**, and enter your license name and key. Premium features unlock immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Lifetime access

Whether you choose a monthly or annual plan, you gain **perpetual access** to any version of Beekeeper Studio released within your subscription period.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Annual subscribers">
    Annual subscribers receive lifetime access immediately upon purchase. If you subscribe on January 1, 2024 and cancel on January 1, 2025, you can use any version released during that period forever — no further payments required.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Monthly subscribers">
    Monthly subscribers receive lifetime access after completing **12 consecutive months**. If you start a monthly subscription on March 10, 2024 and maintain it through March 2025, you have perpetual access to versions released during that window even after you cancel.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Info>
  Lifetime access means you keep your last licensed version forever. You won't receive future updates after your subscription ends, but the version you have continues to work.
</Info>

## Why subscribers stay subscribed

Even after you've earned lifetime access, there are strong reasons to keep your subscription active:

- **20% renewal discount** — After 12 months, your future subscription payments are reduced by 20%.
- **Ongoing software updates** — You get immediate access to all new features and database support as they ship.
- **Cloud workspaces** — Share connections and saved queries with your team via [cloud workspaces](https://beekeeperstudio.io/features/workspace).
- **Guaranteed email support** — Active subscribers receive email support from the Beekeeper Studio team.
- **Price lock** — Your subscription price is locked in even if pricing increases in the future.

## Managing your subscription

You can manage licenses, seats, invoices, and billing details from the account dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to the dashboard">
    Go to [app.beekeeperstudio.io/purchases](https://app.beekeeperstudio.io/purchases) and sign in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View your licenses">
    You'll see all active licenses, their billing cycle, and renewal dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage seats, invoices, or billing">
    - Click **Edit** next to a license to add or remove seats.
    - Click **Billing & Invoices** to access the Stripe portal for invoices and billing address updates.
    - Click **Cancel Subscription** (in red) on a license to end auto-renewal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downloading invoices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to the license management portal">
    Go to [app.beekeeperstudio.io/purchases](https://app.beekeeperstudio.io/purchases).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open billing">
    Click **Billing & Invoices** to open the Stripe billing dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find and download your invoice">
    Scroll to the bottom to see paid invoices. Click any invoice to view and download both the invoice and payment receipt.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Increasing seats

If you need to add users to a license:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to the portal">
    Go to [app.beekeeperstudio.io/purchases](https://app.beekeeperstudio.io/purchases).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the license">
    Click **Edit** next to the license you want to change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update the seat count">
    Change the seat number in the dialog and click **Save**. You will be billed pro-rata for the remainder of the current billing period.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## One-time payments and NET terms

Beekeeper Studio does not offer standard one-time payments. Subscriptions allow the team to continue providing updates for new database releases, operating system changes, and new features.

NET billing is available if you commit to 2 years of the team unlimited plan or purchase an enterprise plan. [Contact sales](mailto:sales@beekeeperstudio.io) for more information.

<Tip>
  You can cancel your subscription at any time. After cancellation, you retain lifetime access to versions released during your subscription period, but you won't receive further updates.
</Tip>
